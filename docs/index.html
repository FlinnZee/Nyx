Everything a messenger should be.
And how it should feel.

Every interaction in Nyx is built twice — once to work flawlessly, once to feel fluid. Spring physics, glass surfaces and light that follows you.

💬

Living conversations

Real-time messaging with sent / delivered / seen ticks, live typing indicators and presence that breathes.

📞

Voice & video calls

Full-screen, peer-to-peer calls with a cinematic in-call experience — mute, camera and speaker at your fingertips.

🎙️

Voice notes

Record with a live waveform, scrub through playback. Your voice, beautifully drawn.

🔒

Private circles

No public directory. Find friends only by exact @handle, connect through requests, join by invite code.

📱

Media stays yours

Photos, files and voice notes live on your devices. The server relays them once — then forgets, forever.

👥

Groups

Bring the whole circle together with named groups, member lists and per-person message colours.

🔔

Always reachable

System notifications when you're away, and a tray presence that keeps Nyx quietly alive.

🪶

Impossibly light

A native, compiled core. The entire experience installs from ~4 MB and starts in a blink.

One app. Three worlds.

Nyx ships with three complete experiences — not color swaps, whole moods. Fonts, motion, light and shape all transform. Tap one — this page will follow.

Built private-first.

Nyx treats your data the way the best messengers do — and goes further with media.

🚪 Invite-only

There is no open sign-up. Every member enters with a one-time invite code minted by someone already inside.

🕵️ Undiscoverable

No browsing, no suggestions, no directory. You exist only to the people who know your exact @handle — and whom you accept.

💨 Relay & forget

Media is relayed just long enough to reach your friend's device, then destroyed on the server. History lives with you.
